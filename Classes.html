Explore Our Fitness Classes

Group Fitness Classes

Get motivated and have fun with our high-energy group fitness classes. Our experienced instructors will lead you through challenging yet accessible workouts designed to burn calories, build strength, and boost your mood. We offer a variety of classes to suit all fitness levels, from beginner to advanced.

Yoga Classes

Find your inner peace and improve your flexibility, strength, and balance with our yoga classes. We offer a variety of styles, from gentle and restorative to more challenging and flowing. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned yogi, there's a class for you. Yoga is a great way to de-stress, improve your posture, and connect with your breath.

Public Pool

The pool is open to everyone

Boxing

This class is designed to teach you the fundamentals of boxing in a safe and controlled environment. You'll learn proper punching technique, footwork, movement, and basic defensive strategies. Expect a high-intensity workout that improves your cardiovascular health, strength, and coordination.

Wrestling

This class will introduce you to the exciting world of wrestling. You'll learn basic takedowns, throws, escapes, and pins in a safe and controlled environment. The class emphasizes technique, conditioning, and developing core strength.

Water Polo group

This class is a fun and energetic introduction to water polo, a team sport played in a pool. It's held at a public pool, making it a great option for those who want to try the sport without needing access to a private facility.
